About Me

I am currently a visiting assistant professor at the University of North Texas. I received my PhD in mathematics at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville in May 2023 as a student of Dr. Marie Jameson.

My research lies in Number Theory, in particular modular forms. I'm interested in their p-adic properties, in studying divisibility of their Fourier coefficients, and in their applications to integer partitions.
